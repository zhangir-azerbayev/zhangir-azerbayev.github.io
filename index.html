Zhangir Azerbayev

I want language models to be capable of complex reasoning and for us to be able to verify their outputs. My research is informed by the following beliefs:

  • Language models will continue to become much smarter in accordance with the scaling laws.
  • A strong base model can become much better at reasoning by learning from experience.
  • The usefulness of a sufficiently smart language model is bottlenecked by our ability to verify its reasoning.
  • Models may become superhuman along some axes while underperforming humans on others. In this regime, studying human-AI interaction will provide inspiration for algorithms that close the loop.
Currently I am a first-year PhD student at Princeton University advised by Prof. Jia Deng.
